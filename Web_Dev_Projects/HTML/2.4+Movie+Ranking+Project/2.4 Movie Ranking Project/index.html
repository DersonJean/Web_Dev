The Best Movies of All Time According to Derson

My top 3 movies of all-time.

I’m in Love with a Church Girl

This movie tells the story of a former drug dealer who falls in love with a devout Christian woman, leading him to question his past and seek redemption through faith.

The Good Doctor

This series follows a young autistic surgeon with savant syndrome as he navigates the challenges of working in a prestigious hospital while proving his value to others.

Sintonia

This Brazilian series explores the interconnected lives of three teenagers growing up in a São Paulo favela, dealing with music, crime, and religion as they chase their dreams.
